About Me

I am a Full Stack Web Developer with a background in Mathematics. My coding journey began with undergraduate and graduate level courses in both Java and SQL, as well as self-learning VBA. Throughout my tenure as a Data Analyst at KPMG, I’ve added value to my teams by developing time-saving and human-error-eliminating Excel Macros for many repeatable processes. I decided to hone my skills at Rutgers University’s Web Development Bootcamp, covering concepts in HTML, CSS, Javascript, and the MERN stack. My greatest strength is creative problem-solving, I am passionate about web technologies, and I have an eagerness for learning.
